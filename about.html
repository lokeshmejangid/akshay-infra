  • Akshay Infrasys is pioneer in primary structural members, has expertise and core competency in Pre-Engineered Steel structures, elevated roads & bridges (medium and heavy hot rolled fabricated Girders, Beams , Columns and H- Beams in standard nominal depth) & building solutions. Akshay Infrasys founded in year 2015 and Its Modern, Technologically advanced plant at Prahladpura, just 15 Kms away from Jaipur Airport, possesses most advance machinery and equipment to produce best quality pre-engineered structures and building solutions.

  • Akshay Infrasys One of the India’s biggest block plants with capacity of around 250 cubic meters equipped with microwave moisture measurement and computer controlled water metering for accuracy.

  • Entire Production Process follows strict Quality Management System that monitors processes at every stage conforming product quality requirements.

  • The plant complies with RDSO standard for Government projects and also customized Designs as per IS800, ASTM, MBMA and AWS standards. The design is Robust and Cost Effective meeting client/ Govt. project requirements with consistency in product reliability.

  • Akshay Infrasys caters to government projects, industrial, commercial and institutional requirements and provide fast delivery with Reduced Time Cycle for Erection/ Commissioning ..

  • Akshay Infrasys manufacture PRE –CAST CONCRETE Blocks which are to be used as an alternative to the red bricks, to Reduce Environmental Pollution and Global warming in commercial, industrial and residential construction as their thermal mass and ability to retain heat, economical, durable and long lasting.

  • Akshay Infrasys manufacturing Paver’s Support Rain Water Harvesting:- Contribute to resolving scarcity of ground water by devising solutions while maintaining flooring aesthetics, by designing a paver system that allows water to seep through the soil and drain water.
