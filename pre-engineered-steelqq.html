Pre-engineered Steel Structures

Pre-engineered steel structures (PEB) offer a cost-effective and flexible solution for various industrial, commercial, and residential applications. These structures are designed and fabricated in advance, using high-grade steel, and then shipped to the site for assembly. The result is a quicker, more efficient construction process compared to traditional building methods.

Advantages of Pre-engineered Steel Structures

  • Reduced construction time and cost
  • Versatile design options for different applications
  • Enhanced durability and resistance to environmental factors
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable building solution

Applications

Pre-engineered steel structures are commonly used in:

  • Warehouses and storage facilities
  • Manufacturing plants
  • Commercial buildings and offices
  • Aircraft hangars
  • Agricultural buildings
