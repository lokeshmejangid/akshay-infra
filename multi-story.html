Multi-storey Steel Structures

  • Single Steel Doors and Window Frames: Strength and Versatility for Modern Spaces

  • Double Steel Doors and Window Frames embody a harmonious fusion of durability, security, and refined design, offering an exceptional solution for both commercial and residential applications. Constructed from high-grade steel, these doors and window frames are engineered to deliver robust performance and resilience against various external factors. The double-door configuration, which features two doors that open in tandem, not only enhances accessibility but also allows for larger entryways. This is particularly advantageous in high-traffic areas or environments where large equipment or goods need to be moved through the openings. The design of the double steel doors ensures that they provide a formidable barrier against forced entry, while also offering enhanced thermal and acoustic insulation.

  • The steel used in these doors and frames is chosen for its strength and longevity, making them well-suited to withstand harsh weather conditions, including heavy rain, strong winds, and extreme temperatures. This durability extends to their resistance against wear and tear, ensuring that the doors and frames maintain their functionality and appearance over time. Additionally, steel’s inherent properties offer excellent protection against fire and impact, making these doors and frames a secure choice for a variety of settings.

  • Aesthetic versatility is another significant feature of double steel doors and window frames. They can be customized in numerous finishes and styles to complement a wide range of architectural themes, from sleek, modern designs to more traditional appearances. The precision-engineered frames ensure a snug fit that enhances their overall performance, including better energy efficiency and noise reduction.

  • Maintenance is straightforward with double steel doors and window frames, thanks to the durability of the material. Steel is resistant to warping, cracking, or swelling, common issues faced by wooden doors and frames. This low-maintenance aspect, combined with the ease of cleaning, makes steel a practical choice for high-traffic or demanding environments. Their ability to provide superior protection, combined with their aesthetic flexibility and minimal maintenance requirements, makes them an excellent choice for enhancing both the functionality and appearance of any building. Whether used in commercial spaces, industrial facilities, or residential properties, these doors and frames deliver long-lasting performance and a refined look.

Preferance of Steel Structure for Multi Stoery Steel Building Construction

Modifiable

Structural steel buildings can be modified in the future for new applications, loading conditions, vertical expansions and changes in owner desires in ways that other framing systems can never accomplish.

Efficient

The typical steel column occupies 75% less floor space than an equivalent concrete column. At the same time structural steel allows longer spans that eliminate intermediate columns creating open floor areas ideal for today’s office layouts.

Reliable and Predictable

Structural steel is manufactured and fabricated under controlled conditions using modern quality assurance processes.The final strength of the material is verified at the point of production, not after the material is already placed in the frame of the building. Structural steel is factory fabricated to close tolerances impossible for site cast.
